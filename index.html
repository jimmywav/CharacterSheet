Your Data Stays Yours
Everything in this app — your tasks, streaks, stats, and history — is stored only on this device. Nothing is sent to a server. Nothing is stored in the cloud. No account required.
A lot of health apps on the App Store collect your data and profit from it. That's not something we're comfortable with. Your habits, routines, and health information are personal — they belong to you, not us.
Use the Export feature to back up your data anytime. If you ever switch devices, you're in full control of what moves with you.
